THE TIMES OF INDIA

How technology is bridging the gaps between modern-day content publishers and business stakeholder

Today, we are undeniably living in an era where innovation, speed and scale are the name of ‘game-change’ for businesses. And for optimally achieving all three and much more, and for standing out amid cut-throat competition in the market, technology is certainly playing a pivotal role across various industry sectors. However, beyond the realm of core business operations and related decision-making as well, technology at large, and tech-enabled interventions in particular, are also in parallel growing in prominence. For instance, technology-usage is leading to multiple breakthroughs off late in terms of redefining media visibility, marketing and content publishing, among other allied aspects that are critical for reputation-building of modern-day companies. That being said, within the content-media-business-connect ecosystem, there are several inadequacies and challenges as of date. To begin with, the ecosystem lacks enough transparency, and additionally, it is unfortunate to note that there is an overdependence on traditional means doing things mostly using outdated techniques and methods. But with the increasing application of technology and tech-led tools, things are changing for the better, thereby allowing businesses to get real-time visibility and insights into the systems and processes involved therein. Speaking of technology bridging and/or at times even eliminating the existing gaps between today’s content publishers (including but not limited to Journalists, Bloggers and Influencers, etc.) and the business stakeholders (including but not limited to CXOs, Regional Directors, Marketing Professionals and HR Leaders, and most importantly, Founders and Business Owners, among others), let’s take a look at the key impact areas in this regard:

  • Community Buidlding
  • Automation
  • Data Analytics

Community Building: In today’s day and age, communities – whether online or offline – are the building blocks of thriving businesses. From building a community virtually to nurturing it effectively, technology acts as a major enabler across the spectrum. Furthermore, tech-enabled data collection can greatly help in attracting and bringing on board like-minded individuals or stakeholders to one’s community, as well as in engaging with the community members to derive insights and best practices.

Automation: From marketing of new products/innovations to social media posting to content creation, and content publishing, to further collaborating and engaging with and across internal and external stakeholders, tech-led automation – enabled by new-age automation tools or software – is nowadays at the forefront of a plethora of reputation-building and public and media relations activities. Not just that automation helps in decreasing human intervention and efforts and lead to improved productivity, but also it can be instrumental in increasing ROI, lead generation and credibility enhancement with the quantum of content being developed and published for business’ visibility.

Data Analytics: Needless to say, nowadays the importance of data-driven business strategy making and content visibility is being well-recognized, but then modern-day businesses often suffer from a double-edged dilemma – either of having too much data (data explosion), most of which may be irrelevant to track or measure; or else, that of having too little data or poor quality, inconsistent data. In solving both of these issues, once again technology is the ultimate savior. With the use of tech-enabled platforms/solutions that provide optimal data collection mechanisms and real-time visibility for business stakeholders through clear and distinctive data analytics, stakeholders are able to identify relevant and well-performing content (that impacts their target groups), and measure/record and constantly measure the data points to track outcomes related to their content, campaigns and so on.
